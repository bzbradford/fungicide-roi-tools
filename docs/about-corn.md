## Corn Fungicide ROI Tool

### About This Calculator

This calculator shares results from university uniform corn fungicide trials conducted in the United States and Canada, and allows farmers and others in the agricultural industry to calculate the potential return on investment (ROI) for corn fungicide application across a variety of user-defined factors.

### What Does Disease Severity Mean?

- **"Low disease"** indicates simulations made using a final end-of-season disease severity level of 1%.
- **"High disease"** indicates simulations made using an end-of-season disease severity level of 5% or above.

These levels were chosen based on previous research demonstrating detectable yield loss from disease at 5% or above. You may also choose to explore a different end-of-season severity by entering a custom disease severity percentage. Disease severity levels should be set at what is expected for a particular hybrid in a particular field at the end of the season.

### About the Data

Data available in the calculator are from university uniform corn fungicide trials conducted across 19 states and Ontario, Canada between 2019 and 2025. Primary diseases in this dataset were tar spot and southern rust. Diseases such as gray leaf spot, northern corn leaf blight, and others were observed at lower frequencies in this dataset.

Learn more about each corn disease:

- Tar Spot - <https://cropprotectionnetwork.org/encyclopedia/tar-spot-of-corn>
- Southern Rust - <https://cropprotectionnetwork.org/encyclopedia/southern-rust-of-corn>
- Gray Leaf Spot - <https://cropprotectionnetwork.org/encyclopedia/gray-leaf-spot-of-corn>
- Northern Corn Leaf Blight - <https://cropprotectionnetwork.org/encyclopedia/northern-corn-leaf-blight-of-corn>
